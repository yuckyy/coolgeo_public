Wedding program:

Day 1
After settling in the hotel and a short rest, you need to register with the notary all the necessary documents for registering the marriage, as well as discuss the wedding details.

Then, we will introduce you to the capital of Georgia - Tbilisi . Walk around the city, accompanied by a guide who will show the main attractions and tell many interesting stories about these places.

Day 2
Morning preparations:
  • For an ideal photo shoot before going out, we suggest you use the professional services of a make-up specialist
  • At your request for the newlyweds will be provided with richly decorated Georgian national costumes.
The artist-photographer is ready to work with you all day. The processed photos will be sent to you in electronic form.

Let's start this day with the registration of marriage in the House of Justice in Tbilisi.

Next, we go to the most beautiful places of Tbilisi, which is famous for its ancient sights as well as new super modern architectural structures: David Agmashenebeli Street, Georgia Trinity Cathedral, walk through Rick Park, Peace Bridge, old quiet streets Tbilisi, Metekhi Bridge, Narikala Fortress and much more…

After a walk around the city, all guests will be invited to a meal in the buffet table. We leave behind magical impressions and amazing views for the photo to the ancient monastery of Jvari. This is one of the most picturesque places in Georgia.

And we will continue the holiday in the ancient city of Mtskheta, where there are many holy and cult places.

By the end of the day we will return to Tbilisi where after a short rest you will be able to rush headlong into the whirlpool of Georgian fun, because only for you and your company in the unique authentic restaurant complex "Melnitsa" (or Keriya, Old Town, Zhamiari ") a real traditional Georgian feast will be organized which is considered to be the cultural heritage of Georgia and has the status of a monument of intangible culture.

Day 3
Free day for walking around Tbilisi. The hotel is located in the heart of the city, so you will easily get to the most interesting places to buy souvenirs, stroll through the narrow streets of the old city and enjoy the atmosphere of Georgia
